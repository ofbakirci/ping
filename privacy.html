PRIVACY POLICY

PING does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Full stop.

No data leaves your device

The app makes no network requests. It has no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no accounts, and no third-party services or SDKs of any kind.

What is stored, and where

The only thing PING saves is your level progress — how far into the dark you've gone. It is kept locally on your device, in the app's own storage. It never leaves your device, and deleting the app removes it.

Your rights

Because PING collects nothing, there is nothing for us to hold, share, sell, request, correct, or delete. There is no data anywhere but on your own device.
